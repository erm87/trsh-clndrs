How to find your collection schedule

  1. Go to the Mecklenburg County GeoPortal
  2. Enter your home address and select the correct match from the matched results
  3. Click on the Trash & Recycling option which will scroll you down to that section.
  4. Shown is your trash and recycling collection day plus your recycling pickup color (orange or green).

Charlotte Trash Pickup Details

  • Trash is collected on weekdays M-F
  • Recycling is collected every other week depending on your color code (Green or Orange)
  • Observed holidays, all collection services operate on a one-day delay with Friday customers serviced on Saturday

These schedules are for the 2022 year and runs until the week of December 31. I’ll update for 2023 as the City of Charlotte posts next year’s schedules closer to the end of the year.

What’s the Recycling color code for?

All of Charlotte operates on the Green/Orange every-other-week schedule for recycling.

Does the calendar send notifications?

By default, I’ve set a single notification per week the evening before the collection day at 6 pm ET. This can be customized to your liking.
